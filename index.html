Brendan O'Callaghan Ratliff - composer, sound engineer and game developer. I'm also Audio Director at Cardboard Sword where we've just released The Siege and The Sandfox and are working on the forthcoming game Transmission.
I specialise in:
  • Game music, sound design & audio production
  • Design & implementation of realtime dynamic/procedural game music systems in Unreal Engine (C++ and Blueprint); at Cardboard Sword I write and maintain our in-house audio middleware, including a standalone DAW and various Unreal Engine plugins.
  • General gameplay and game systems coding; dev tooling; build engineering
  • Location sound recording & audio post production for TV, film, radio and podcast
